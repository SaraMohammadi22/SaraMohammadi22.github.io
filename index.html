Welcome to my Page!

I am a doctoral candidate at Imperial College Business School, Analytics and Operations Department, advised by Dr. Reza Skandari and Prof. Marisa Miraldo. I am also affiliated with the Centre for Health Economics and Policy Innovation (CHEPI). My research focuses on sequential decision-making and data-driven optimization, particularly within healthcare systems and medical applications. My recent work focuses on optimizing clinical decision-making, enhancing healthcare operations through dynamic programming, and designing data-driven models for efficient resource allocation, all aimed at improving patient outcomes by developing personalized, tailored policies.
